About the author


Diego Velasco is a 29-year-old Civil Engineer from Montevideo, Uruguay. Though a frustrated guitarist, he is nonetheless passionate about music and constantly trying to discover new artists, genres, podcasts, books, and pretty much almost everything which may lead to any music previously undiscovered. Be it working at the office, commuting, excercising, or simply kicking back to the sound of a vinyl rolling around the turntable, music takes up a big part of his life, which is why the idea of a music blog in which the users could discuss anything and everything on the subject seemed only natural. Personal favourites include Indie, Shoegaze, Dream Pop, Trip Hop, Rap, Psychedelic and Progressive Rock, but any topic is up for discussion. As the the logo states, if it can be listened to, it can be talked about!
